Welcome to Jeopardy! Here's how to play:

The board will show a series of categories,
each with clues that increase in difficulty.
Click on a dollar value to reveal the answer for a clue;
it's up to you to ask the question it belongs to!
For example, if the given answer was
"A flat boat made by tying together pieces of wood,"
then the expected question would be
"What is a raft?"
When ready, click the answer to reveal
the content required for its question.
If you'd like to check the prompt again,
another click will flip the clue for you.
